Our second attempt at profiling will look at the possibility of parallelising the evaluation of a different pair of values. Instead of evaluating the right and left sub - sorts in parallel, let us look at trying to generate the right and left sub - lists (prior to sorting) in parallel.

In fact, we know in advance that this will not work, because of the serial nature of the partition function. We will proceed anyway, to verify this conjecture.
